Attributes
=======================================
strength	weight carried, small increase in hp, increases damage
agility		walking speed, attack speed, casting speed, hit chance, dodge chance
constitution	health
intelligence	increases experience gained, magic damage, improves skill maxima, small MP effect
wisdom		increases magic effectiveness (duration of effects, amount of effects), large MP effect


Classes
=======================================
warrior		mostly constitution/strength - fighting power, a little magic
thief		mostly agility/constitution/intelligence - a fighter with speed and some magic
artisan		even builds are best - they can gain select abilities of any class via items
paladin		warrior with significant magic abilities
cleric		mage focused on lots of magic; lowest HP of any class
summoner	mage focused on passive attacks; second-lowest HP
warlock		battle mage with a little less HP and a little more MP than a wizard
wizard		a battle-mage with a little more HP than a warlock, but a little less MP

Equations
=======================================
current level's HP = last level's HP + (constitution + strength / 3) * classHPModifier * (level / (level+1))
current level's MP = last level's MP + (wisdom + intelligence / 3)   * classMPModifier * (level / (level+1))
